Hainanese Chicken Rice

A comforting and flavorful dish featuring tender poached chicken served with aromatic rice, accompanied by chili sauce and ginger-garlic sauce.

Prep Time: 30 minutes

Cook Time: 1 hour

Servings: 2

Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken (about 1.5 kg)
  • 5 cm piece of ginger, sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, smashed
  • 1 stalk spring onion (scallion)
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil
  • 2 tablespoons chicken fat or vegetable oil
  • 2 cups jasmine rice, rinsed
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons oyster sauce
  • 2 tablespoons dark soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons light soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons chicken broth
  • 2 tablespoons lime juice
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • 2 tablespoons chili paste
  • 2 tablespoons garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons ginger, minced
  • 2 tablespoons cilantro, chopped
  • 2 cucumbers, sliced

Instructions

  1. Clean the chicken by rubbing it with salt and rinsing under cold water.
  2. In a large pot, add the whole chicken, ginger slices, garlic cloves, spring onion, and enough water to submerge the chicken.
  3. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for about 1 hour, or until the chicken is fully cooked.
  4. Once cooked, remove the chicken and set aside to cool. Reserve the broth for cooking the rice and making the soup.
  5. In a pan, heat the chicken fat or vegetable oil over medium heat. Add chopped garlic and grated ginger, sautéing until fragrant.
  6. Add the rinsed rice to the pan, stirring to coat the grains with the aromatic oil.
  7. Transfer the rice to a rice cooker, add the reserved chicken broth, soy sauce, and cook according to the rice cooker's instructions.
  8. For the chili sauce, blend chili paste, garlic, ginger, lime juice, sugar, and water until smooth. Adjust seasoning to taste.
  9. For the ginger-garlic sauce, sauté minced garlic and ginger in sesame oil until fragrant. Add soy sauce and chicken broth, simmering for a few minutes.
  10. Serve the poached chicken with the aromatic rice, chili sauce, ginger-garlic sauce, and sliced cucumbers.

Tips

  • For extra flavor, use chicken broth instead of water to cook the rice.
  • Adjust the spiciness of the chili sauce by adding more or less chili paste.
  • Garnish with chopped cilantro for added freshness.
